Remember: Your payoff is jointly determined by
• whether a red or a green ball is drawn from an urn containing 20 red and 80 green balls,
• whether you choose A or B, and
• whether your co-player chooses A or B.

If a green ball is drawn, and:
• you choose A, and your co-player chooses A: You receive US$ 1. Your co-player receives US$ 1.
• you choose A, and your co-player chooses B: You receive US$ 1. Your co-player receives US$ 3.
• you choose B, and your co-player chooses A: You receive US$ 3. Your co-player receives US$ 1.
• you choose B, and your co-player chooses B: You receive nothing (US$ 0). Your co-player receives nothing (US$ 0).

If a red ball is drawn, you and your co-player both receive nothing (US$ 0).

On average, a computer chooses A in about 60 out of 100 cases and B in about 40 out of 100 cases.
