Remember: Your payoff is jointly determined by:

  • whether a red or a green ball is drawn from an urn containing 20 red and 80 green balls,
  • whether the action implemented for you is A or B, and
  • whether your co-player chooses A or B.

If a green ball is drawn, and:

  • the action implemented for you is A, and your co-player chooses A: You receive US$ 1. Your co-player receives US$ 1.
  • the action implemented for you is A, and your co-player chooses B: You receive US$ 1. Your co-player receives US$ 3.
  • the action implemented for you is B, and your co-player chooses A: You receive US$ 3. Your co-player receives US$ 1.
  • the action implemented for you is B, and your co-player chooses B: You receive nothing (US$ 0). Your co-player receives nothing (US$ 0).

If a red ball is drawn, you and your co-player both receive nothing (US$ 0).
